About

Currently I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Faculty of Mathematics and Statistics of University of St. Gallen. My research interests lie at the interface of stochastic processes, mathematical finance and machine learning. My research is funded by Juan-Pablo Ortega's SNF project Novel Architectures for Photonic Reservoir Computing . Previously I was a postdoctoral researcher at D-MATH of ETH Zürich funded by Josef Teichmann's SNF project Mathematical Finance in the light of machine learning.
